Most vendor delays come from poor visibility, not intent.

## Why delays happen

- No standard RFQ format
- Hidden dependency on outsourced sub-processes
- Weak update cadence during execution

## How Axis Connect helps

- Standardized job posting and quote templates
- Verified vendors with delivery track records
- Stage-wise status updates and notifications
