ISO 9001:2015 is often seen as a certificate on the wall. In practice, it is a **discipline framework** for repeatability, risk control, and customer confidence.

## Why it matters on the shop floor

- Standardized process documentation
- Controlled change management
- Traceable inspection records

> At Axis Micro, ISO principles are embedded in routing, inspection plans, and final dispatch checks.

## Common misconception

ISO does not guarantee zero defects. It guarantees a **system that detects, controls, and continuously improves**.
