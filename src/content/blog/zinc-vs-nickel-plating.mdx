Selecting plating is a trade-off between performance and cost.

## Zinc plating

- Cost-effective for broad commercial use
- Suitable for moderate corrosion environments

## Nickel plating

- Better wear and corrosion resistance
- Preferred in harsher or high-performance environments

## Decision factors

1. Service environment
2. Required lifecycle
3. Cost target
